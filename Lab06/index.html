Welcome to My Village Saroba

About My Village

My village name is Saroba. It is located near Chakri Interchange. The people of Saroba are kind, hardworking, and most of them are farmers. The environment here is calm and refreshing, surrounded by beautiful fields and trees.Life in Saroba is simple yet fulfilling. Early in the morning, you can hear the birds singing and see the farmers heading to their fields. The air is fresh, and the people live close to nature.

Saroba is known for its strong sense of community. Neighbors help each other in times of need, and everyone takes part in village decisions. Weddings, festivals, and local gatherings bring everyone together in a spirit of unity and happiness.

The village is surrounded by natural beauty — wide green fields, small canals, and tall trees along the roads. The fresh breeze and peaceful atmosphere make Saroba an ideal place for relaxation and family life.

In recent years, development projects have improved roads, schools, and healthcare facilities. However, the people still preserve their traditions and continue to live close to nature.
