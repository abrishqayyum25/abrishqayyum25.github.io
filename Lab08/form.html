Welcome to My Village Saroba

About My Village

My village name is Saroba. It is located near Chakri Interchange. The people of Saroba are kind, hardworking, and most of them are farmers. The environment here is calm and refreshing, surrounded by beautiful fields and trees.Life in Saroba is simple yet fulfilling. Early in the morning, you can hear the birds singings.and air is very fresh

Green Fields

Our village is surrounded by wide green fields that give peace to the eyes and heart.

  • Fresh crops
  • Beautiful scenery
  • Healthy air

Local Festivals

People celebrate festivals with joy, unity, and traditional music and food.

  • Cultural songs
  • Traditional food
  • Community spirit
